Our Story

Experience and taste freshly brewed coffee. Coffee beans that are prouldy produce locally. Beans that are carefully picked from one of the well known Organic Coffee Farm located at the down hills of Mt. Banahaw. The good weather and richnest of the mountain soil, affects a lot the quality of the Coffee beans. Barako's Cafe offers the classic taste of coffee that is, best paired with our own recipe of freshly made goodies. We are also now serving the classic set of snacks and drinks.
